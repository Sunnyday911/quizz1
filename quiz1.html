Sidoarjo

Sidoarjo is a regency in East Java, Indonesia, located just south of Surabaya. It is known for its high population density and serves as a key suburban area supporting Surabaya. The region is bordered by Surabaya and Gresik to the north, Pasuruan to the south, Mojokerto to the west, and the Madura Strait to the east. As of mid-2023, the population of Sidoarjo Regency is estimated to be around 1,996,825.
